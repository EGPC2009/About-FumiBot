About the FumiBot

Versions

FumiBot V1 is the first functioning version of the FumiBot. It is an innovative disinfectant robot designed to fumigate ventilation shafts through user control or algorithm. It was created during the school year 2023-2024 by the Grade 9 students of Group 2 from the class section Pearl.
FumiBot V2 is the second functioning version of the FumiBot. It is an innovative disinfectant robot designed to clean flat surfaces such as floors. It was created during the school year 2024-2025 by the Grade 10 students of Group 1 from the class section Peace.
